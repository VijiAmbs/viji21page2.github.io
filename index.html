TIPS & TRICKS

Make kickboxing more fun!

While kickboxing can be an intensive sport, posture and technique goes a long way in throwing a stronger punch with minimal physical impact. Here are some really cool tips & tricks you should follow while taking up the sport.

Always keep your hands up

Never take your eyes off your partner

Retract your punches back to your chin for your protection.

Keep your feet in the ground at all times

Start at a slow and comfortable pace to reduce fear and flinching.

Throw combinations, the more you throw, the more you land.
